## Root Cause

During AutoRound's calibration forward pass, the attention mask tensor passed to torch.nn.functional.scaled_dot_product_attention (SDPA) has dtype `long int` instead of the required `bool`, `float`, or `Half`. SDPA enforces strict dtype checking and rejects integer attention masks when running in fp16/bf16 mixed precision.
