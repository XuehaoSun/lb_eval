## Root Cause

A single 44.52 GiB GPU is insufficient to load the 27B-parameter Qwen model in fp16 for AutoRound W4A16 quantization. Model weights alone require ~54 GiB in fp16, and the materialize-copy step in transformers' parallel state-dict loading cannot allocate even an extra 170 MiB because the GPU is already saturated at 44.44/44.52 GiB.
